Studio

Swift architects studio founded in 2005
We are a team of creative and innovative architects.

Master Planning

Swift Architects are strategic thinkers who work to ground complex landscape planning and large scale masterplan projects through engagement with local communities and character ecology.

Residential

Swift Architects have extensive experience in designing for residential developments with shared and private spaces, often with tight constraints and specific client needs.

Landscaping Design

Our studio foundation lies in designing, constructing and developing high quality, personal and memorable private gardens, parks and estates, with detailed planting schemes.

People

Swift Architects is a team of collaborative, innovative thinkers who enjoy contributing to our dynamic, rewarding studio culture.

Team

We’ve come together from many different backgrounds, united by our passion for working critically and with integrity and curiosity.
